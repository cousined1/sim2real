### Problem section

**Section heading:**  
The simulator is not your deployment environment.

**Body copy:**  
Most robotics models fail in production for reasons that do not show up in clean synthetic training data. Lighting shifts, worn surfaces, box tilt, clutter, friction variance, dust, and sensor noise create failure patterns that standard simulation workflows miss.

Teams often respond by collecting more real-world data manually, but that process is slow, expensive, and difficult to scale across changing environments. Sim2Real closes that gap by learning directly from deployment failures and using them to improve future simulation runs.

### Solution section

**Section heading:**  
A feedback loop from the real world back into training

**Intro:**  
Sim2Real captures what actually happened during robot execution, compares it to what the simulator expected, and converts those mismatches into structured improvements.

**Three-step flow:**

**1. Capture deployment telemetry**  
Collect camera, force-torque, IMU, and task outcome data from real robot operations.

**2. Detect failure causes**  
Identify where simulation assumptions diverged from actual conditions, including object geometry variance, lighting, friction, tilt, clutter, and contact behavior.

**3. Improve future training**  
Generate better simulation parameter sets and synthetic scenarios so training reflects real deployment conditions more closely.

### Features section

**Section heading:**  
Core product capabilities

**Feature 1: Real-world calibration layer**  
Passively ingests deployment telemetry and compares expected vs. observed outcomes.

**Feature 2: Failure analysis engine**  
Classifies failure patterns and surfaces repeatable sim-to-real gaps by task, environment, and object type.

**Feature 3: Simulation perturbation generator**  
Transforms observed discrepancies into updated simulation conditions such as friction, clutter density, lighting variance, and object pose randomness.

**Feature 4: AI reality translator**  
Uses vision and language models to describe real-world scenes in structured terms the simulator can use.

**Feature 5: Closed feedback loop**  
Turns failed attempts into better synthetic training data for future model improvement.

**Feature 6: Deployment insights dashboard**  
Shows failure trends, transfer bottlenecks, task performance, and recommended simulation updates over time.
